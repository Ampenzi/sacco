Our Products

Purpose
Development loans address the development needs of members e.g. to acquire property and other assets, start or boost business, undertake large scale farming, motor vehicle purchase among others.
  1. A member can access only one development loan at a time
  2. Repayment period shall not exceed 36 months.
  3. Interest rate chargeable shall be 12%.
  4. Maximum entitlement shall be 3 times of total deposits and Shares
  5. Security options: Guarantors’ deposits, member deposits

Emergency loans enable our members to pay for emergencies e.g. hospital bills.
  1. Repayable within 12 months.
  2. Maximum Amount 30,000
  3. Security options: Guarantors’ deposits, member deposits.
  4. Interest rate is 1% reducing balance basis

School Fees loan capped to KES 200,000. The proposed terms of the product will be as follows;
  1. Repayable within 12 months.
  2. Must be secured by other members.
  3. Interest rate is 1% reducing balance basis.

  1. Up to 95% of deposits
  2. Member must not be a guarantor
  3. Interest is 1% per month on a reducing balance

Loan Eligibility

A member must have been in the sacco for at least six (6) months, and must have contributed the minimum amount to the sacco in shares and made deposit of any amount.

Loaning Process

  1. Fill the official Uthabiti Sacco loan application form.
  2. Unless the loan applied for is less or equal to a members total savings, it must be secured by guarantors. The total shares and deposits of the borrower and those of the guarantors, must be equal to or more than the loan request.
  3. All members must be members of Uthabiti Sacco
