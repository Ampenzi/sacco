Get to know about Uthabiti Sacco

Uthabiti Sacco Society Ltd was founded in 14th June 2014 as Savings and credit cooperative Operates Back Office Service Activity Has 300 registered members Has 9 board Members and 3 supervisory members Registered office Deliverance Church Kahawa Sukari Has different loan products ranging from school fees, development, emergency loans Holds AGM every year Regulated by ministry of cooperative development (Ruiru sub county cooperative officer)
